Our Story

Every Wall Is
a Canvas

Jensen Decors is a curated marketplace for premium wall art and decorative pieces. We believe every wall is a canvas — and the right piece can define the character of an entire space.

From intimate home studies to grand corporate lobbies, every product we carry is selected for quality, artistry, and lasting impression. What started as a small showroom in Karachi has grown into one of Pakistan's most trusted names in interior wall décor.

We partner directly with skilled artisans and leading international brands to bring you pieces that are not just beautiful, but built to last. Each product in our collection is handpicked by our in-house design team.

How We Got Here

Our Journey

2005
The Beginning
Jensen Decors opened its first showroom in Karachi with a small but carefully selected range of metal wall art and decorative mirrors.
2009
Expanding the Collection
We introduced wallpapers, photo frame art, and wall shelves — growing our catalogue to over 40 products and partnering with our first international brand.
2013
Second Showroom
Demand grew beyond Karachi. We opened our second location and signed distribution agreements with Gloster and Nordic House.
2018
Going Digital
Jensen Decors launched its online presence, making our full catalogue accessible across Pakistan and bringing the showroom experience to every home.
2023
4,000 Happy Clients
Eighteen years in, we crossed 4,000 satisfied clients and 127 products — and we're just getting started. The walls of Pakistan deserve the best.
